FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is the buying process for properties?

Our buying process is simple and transparent. First, browse our property listings and shortlist your preferences. Then, schedule a site visit. Once you've made your decision, we handle all documentation, legal formalities, and registration. Our team guides you through every step.

Do you provide home loan assistance?

Yes, we provide complete home loan assistance. We have tie-ups with leading banks and financial institutions to help you get the best interest rates and quick loan approval. Our team will guide you through the entire loan application process.

What documents are required for property registration?

For property registration, you'll need identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, passport-size photographs, income proof (for loan cases), and the sale agreement. Our legal team will provide you with a complete checklist and assist with all documentation.

What are the EMI options available?

We offer flexible EMI options ranging from 5 to 30 years. The EMI amount depends on the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. We can help you calculate the exact EMI based on your requirements and financial capacity.

Can I schedule a site visit before booking?

Absolutely! We encourage site visits before making any booking decision. You can schedule a visit through our website, by phone, or via WhatsApp. We'll arrange a convenient time and our agent will accompany you to show the property.

Are the properties legally verified?

Yes, all our properties are thoroughly verified by our legal team. We check title deeds, encumbrance certificates, approval from relevant authorities, and all necessary legal documents before listing any property.

What payment plans do you offer?

We offer various payment plans including down payment, construction-linked plans, and post-dated cheque plans. For under-construction properties, we have flexible payment schedules aligned with construction milestones.

When will I get possession of the property?

Possession timelines vary by project. For ready-to-move properties, possession is immediate. For under-construction projects, we provide a clear timeline in the agreement. We keep you updated on construction progress throughout.

What about maintenance charges?

Maintenance charges vary based on the property type and amenities offered. We provide a clear breakdown of all maintenance costs before booking. Most properties have a maintenance fund managed by the residents' association.

Is real estate a good investment?

Real estate is considered one of the safest and most profitable investment options. Property values typically appreciate over time, providing both capital appreciation and rental income. Our experts can help you choose properties with high investment potential.
